Brisket Sandwich Recipe

If you need a brisket recipe, click here.

Ingredients You Will Need:

  • Brioche bun
  • Brisket
  • BBQ sauce
  • Sweet pickle slices
  1. Cut up smoked brisket.
  2. Coat inside of bun with BBQ sauce.
  3. Add brisket to bun liberally.
  4. Add pickles to the sandwich.
  5. Consume!
