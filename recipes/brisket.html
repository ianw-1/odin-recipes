The Smoked Brisket Recipe for Your Sandwich!

A brisket is fairly simple, you just need a few ingredients.

Ingredients for a smoked brisket:

  • 1lb of prime beef brisket
  • Salt
  • Pepper
  • Garlic powder
  • Butcher paper

Steps to prepare:

  1. Trim brisket to your liking.
  2. Season with salt, pepper, and garlic powder.
  3. Wrap brisket in butcher paper to keep all the juices in.
  4. Smoke at 225 degrees until the internal temperature is 202 degrees.
  5. Rest brisket for 1-2 hours.
  6. Slice brisket for sandwiches!
