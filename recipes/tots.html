Tater Tots Recipe

These are super sipmle, we are going to start with a bag of the Ore-Ida frozen tots and an air fryer.

Ingredients Needed:

  • Ore-Ida tater tots (I like the mini tots.)
  • Creole seasoning of your choice
  • Cooking spray

Steps

  1. Prep your air fryer to 425
  2. Put Ore-Ida tots in the basket evenly spread
  3. Spray the cooking spray thinly on the tots
  4. Shake the creole seasoning evenly on the tater tots
  5. Set air fryer time for 7 minutes and cook the tots
  6. Let the tater tots rest for 1 minute
  7. Serve with main course
